A Time Line of the Life of Michael Jordan:

  • 1963 - Born on February 17th in Brooklyn, NY
  • 1979 - Gets cut from the varsity team at Laney High in North Carolina
  • 1981 - After two Varsity season Michael is selcted McDonalds All American, and accepts a scholarship to The University of North Carolina
  • 1984 - Selected Number 3 overall by the Chicago Bulls in the NBA Draft
  • 1985 - Selected rookie of the year after averaging 28.2 points, 6.5 rebounds, and 5.9 assists
  • 1985 - After signing a shoe deal with Nike, the Air Jordan 1 basketball shoe is released
  • 1991 - Jordan wins his first NBA Championship, after defeating a very good Lakers team
  • 1994 - Jordan signs a contract baseball in the MLB for the Chicago White Sox
  • 2003 - Jordan plays his last game in the NBA scoring 15 points
  • 2010 - Jordan buys majority ownership of the Charlotte Bobcats, now known as the hornets for $275 million
